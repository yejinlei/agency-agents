## What is NEXUS?

**NEXUS** (Network of EXperts, Unified in Strategy) turns The Agency's 人工智能 specialists into a coordinated pipeline. Instead of activating agents one at a time and hoping they work together, NEXUS defines exactly who does what, when, and how quality is verified at every step.

## 🔑 Key Concepts in 30 Seconds

1. **Quality Gates** — No phase advances without evidence-based approval
2. **Dev↔QA Loop** — Every task is built then tested; PASS to proceed, F人工智能L to retry (max 3)
3. **交接s** — Structured context transfer between agents (never start cold)
4. **Reality Checker** — Final quality authority; defaults to "NEEDS WORK"
5. **Agents Orchestrator** — Pipeline controller 管理 the entire flow
6. **Evidence Over Claims** — Screenshots, test results, and data — not assertions
